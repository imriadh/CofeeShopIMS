COFFEE SHOP MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
=============================

Installation Instructions:
--------------------------
1. Double-click "Setup.bat".
   - This will automatically create a "Coffee Shop App" shortcut on your Desktop.

2. Go to your Desktop and double-click "Coffee Shop App".
